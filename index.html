Typography

We pair New Spirit (serif) for display and headings with Inter (sans-serif) for body and UI text. New Spirit brings warmth and personality; Inter provides clarity and readability.

Spacing

A consistent 4px-based spacing scale used throughout the design system for margins, padding, and gaps.

Shape / Border Radius

Consistent border radii create a cohesive feel. Buttons use a full pill shape while cards and inputs use softer rounding.

Elevation

Six elevation levels using box shadows to create depth and hierarchy in the interface.

Buttons

All buttons use a pill shape (48px border-radius) for a friendly, approachable feel. Three variants — Contained, Outlined, and Text — each with Primary, Secondary, and Tertiary colors.

Section Themes

Themed sections use 3 emphasis levels across 5 color families. High emphasis uses vivid mid-tones, medium uses pale tints, and low emphasis swaps the serif heading for sans-serif.

Cards

Cards are the primary content container. White background, 8px radius, with three padding variants based on content density.

Small Variant

No outer padding. Content fills the card edge-to-edge with inner sections padded independently.

padding: 0px

Medium Variant

Standard card padding for lists and grids where space is at a premium.

padding: 16px

Large Variant

Generous padding for featured content, hero cards, and prominent placements.

padding: 20px

Brand Voice & Tone

iGraal speaks to Polish users with warmth, clarity, and confidence. Every message should feel like advice from a savvy friend.

🤝

Friendly & Approachable

We use direct, conversational language. No corporate jargon. We're a friend who knows how to save money.

🛡️

Trust & Transparency

We lead with trust signals — 20 years of experience, 12M+ happy users, clear rules, verified offers. No hidden catches.

🚀

Action-Oriented

CTAs are direct and benefit-driven: "Dołącz i zyskaj 12zł" (Join and earn 12zł). Every action has a clear reward.

✨

Empowering

We celebrate smart shopping. Our users aren't penny-pinchers — they're savvy people who know the value of their money.
